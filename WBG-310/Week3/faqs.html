Devil May Cry Game Series FAQ

What is Devil May Cry?

Devil May Cry is an action-adventure hack and slash video game series created by Hideki Kamiya and developed by Capcom. The series focuses on the main character, Dante, a demon hunter with supernatural abilities.

How many games are there in the Devil May Cry series?

The Devil May Cry series currently consists of [7] games, including titles like Devil May Cry, Devil May Cry 2, Devil May Cry 3, Devil May Cry 4 and Devil May Cry 5. This List dont add the deluxe Editon or Special Edition

Can I play Devil May Cry on multiple platforms?

Yes, Devil May Cry games are available on various gaming platforms, including [PS4,PS5, Xbox]. Make sure to check the system requirements for each game.
