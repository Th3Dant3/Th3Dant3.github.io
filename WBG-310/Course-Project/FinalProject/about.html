About Diablo

Diablo is a series of action role-playing hack and slash video games developed by Blizzard Entertainment. The series is set in the fictional world of Sanctuary and focuses on the battle between the forces of Heaven and Hell.

Diablo

Diablo was released in 1996 and is the first game in the series. It follows the story of a lone hero battling the Lord of Terror, Diablo, as he descends into the town of Tristram.

Timeline: 1996

Diablo II

Diablo II was released in 2000 and continues the story of the original game. Players must journey across the deserts of Aranoch and the jungles of Kurast to defeat Diablo's brothers, Mephisto and Baal.

Timeline: 2000

Diablo III

Diablo III was released in 2012 and features a new generation of heroes fighting against the Prime Evil, Diablo, who has returned to wreak havoc on Sanctuary.

Timeline: 2012

Diablo IV

Diablo IV is the upcoming installment in the series, announced in 2019. It returns to the series' dark and gothic roots, with players battling against the forces of Hell in an open-world environment.
